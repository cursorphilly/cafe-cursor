# Cafe Cursor

Build together, leave with something.

Cafe Cursor is a relaxed daytime co-work session for developers, designers, and founders using [Cursor](https://cursor.com). No talks, no strict agenda — bring your laptop, grab food or drinks, and ship alongside other builders in Philly.

## Featured starter: AR zine

The physical zine you received at Cafe Cursor is a **WebAR zine** — point your phone at a page and AR layers pop up. Fork the starter and add your own page:

**[ar-zine-starter](https://github.com/luiscielak/ar-zine-starter)**
