# Cursor credits

Limited Cursor credits are available for Cafe Cursor attendees — **first come, first served** at check-in.

## How it works

1. **Check in** when you arrive. A volunteer will confirm your RSVP.
2. **Ask for credits** at the check-in table if you want a link.
3. **Open the link** on your own device — each link is single-use and assigned to one person.
4. **Redeem** by following the prompts in the link. If anything fails, ask at check-in or message on Discord.

## Important

- Credit links are **not** printed on the zine or posted publicly — you get yours individually at the event.
- Links expire; redeem soon after you receive one.
- Credits are a thank-you perk, not a ticket requirement. The event is free.

## Issues?

Reply to the email you received, or ask in the [Cursor Philly Discord](https://discord.gg/qXPAZNRR3T).
