## What is Cafe Cursor?

A relaxed daytime co-work session for builders to share what you're shipping and meet other Cursor users in Philly.

This is **not** a talk night or a formal workshop. It's an open space to build together: bring your laptop, grab food or drinks, and co-work through the afternoon on a side project, debug with someone new, or swap workflows with other developers, designers, and founders.

Whether you're looking for a chill spot to make progress on something you've been putting off, or you just want to hang out and meet the community — this is the place.
